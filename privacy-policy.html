🌙 Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Cuentos de Luna ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (name and email) from these providers.

Child Profile Information: To personalize stories, the App allows parents to store the child's name, age range, and gender. This information is stored locally and in your account profile, and is used solely for story personalization.

Guest Mode: You may generate one story without creating an account. In guest mode, no personal data is stored on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as stories generated, app sessions, and feature usage through PostHog (EU cloud) to improve our service. We also collect error reports through Sentry to identify and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized bedtime stories using AI technology
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To improve and optimize the App experience
  • To communicate important updates about the service
  • To monitor and fix errors and crashes

3. AI Story Generation & COPPA Compliance

Stories are generated using Anthropic's Claude AI. The child's name is never sent to our servers or to the AI service. Instead, we use a placeholder ([NOMBRE]) in the prompt sent to the AI, and the actual name is substituted client-side on your device after the story is generated. Only the child's age range, preferred language, interests, and story preferences are sent to the AI service to create personalized content. No data is retained by the AI service after story generation. We do not use this data to train AI models.

4. Content Safety

Cuentos de Luna implements a multi-layer content safety system to ensure all stories are appropriate for children:

  • Client-side filtering: User inputs (child name, custom interests) are validated in real-time against a blocklist of inappropriate content in 8 languages.
  • Server-side filtering: All user-provided fields are validated again on our servers before being sent to the AI.
  • AI-level safeguards: The AI prompt includes explicit instructions to generate only age-appropriate, child-friendly content.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures including Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Cuentos de Luna is designed for use by parents/guardians to create stories for their children. We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
  • All account creation and data input is performed by the parent or guardian.
  • The child's name is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party service.
  • The child's age range and preferences are provided by the parent solely for story personalization.
  • Parents can manage, edit, or delete child profiles at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and data storage
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Story generation
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing
  • PostHog (EU cloud): Anonymous product analytics
  • Sentry: Error tracking and crash reporting

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the data they process. PostHog data is hosted in the EU to comply with European data protection regulations.

8. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information (credit card numbers, bank details). Subscription management is handled through RevenueCat, which receives only anonymized transaction data.

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all personal data including child profiles and generated stories will be permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers. Analytics data (PostHog) is processed within the EU.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
